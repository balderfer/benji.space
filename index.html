In 2018 my therapist challenged me to make a visual representation of who I wanted to become.

I thought about how I wanted to be like my grandmother, someone who is accepting of and interested in all perspectives.

I thought about how I wanted to be fluid. I wanted to be able to go with the flow yet make my own flow.

I thought about how I didn't want to box myself in to any predefined definition.

The mark to the left is what I came up with and it makes me happy.
